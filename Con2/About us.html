About us

Know more about International Conference on
Industry Institutional Futuristic Research in Engineering.

The 1st International Conference on Industry Institutional Futuristic Research in Engineering (I2FRE) is to be held at School of Engineering, P P Savani University (PPSU), Surat, India on January 08, 2022.

The conference topic is multifaceted which covers the applications of modern techniques and technologies in Engineering Sciences. This conference will bring multidisciplinary work in a single platform by integrating the enhanced technologies of various fields. This will encourage graduates, academicians, researchers, scientists and industrialists to discuss in common arena about the applications of technology which demands to meet for present society. The conference will bring an opportunity to students, academicians, research scholars, scientists and industry personnel to contribute their work in the domain of chemical, civil, mechanical, information technology and computer science. Presented and accepted papers will be published in SCI-indexed Journals/ Scopus indexed Journals. Conference will be conducted in both Online as well as Offline Mode.
